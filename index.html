Profile

    I have been a research fellow at the Trinity College Dublin since November 2017, in a project of exploring Deep Neural Networks (DNN) optimisation for efficient inference on resource-constraint systems. Before that, I was a postdoc researcher at the University of Edinburgh, from where I received my PhD in the topic of task scheduling for CPU-GPU heterogeneous platforms. My current research involves AI compiler, DNNs optimisation and heterogeneous computing.
